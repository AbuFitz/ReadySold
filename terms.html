1. Introduction

Welcome to ReadySold. These Terms and Conditions govern your use of our website and services. By using our services, you agree to be bound by these terms.

2. Definitions

In these Terms and Conditions:

  • "We," "us," or "our" refers to ReadySold
  • "You" or "your" refers to the user of our services
  • "Vehicle" refers to the car you wish to sell
  • "Valuation" refers to the price quote we provide for your vehicle
  • "Services" refers to our car buying and valuation services

3. Our Services

3.1 Valuation Service

We provide instant online valuations for vehicles. These valuations are indicative and subject to physical inspection of the vehicle.

3.2 Vehicle Purchase

We offer to purchase vehicles based on the information you provide. The final purchase price is subject to verification during inspection.

3.3 Collection Service

We provide free vehicle collection services across the UK mainland. Collection times are subject to availability and scheduling.

4. Valuation Process

4.1 Accuracy of Information

You must provide accurate and truthful information about your vehicle, including:

  • Registration number
  • Mileage
  • Condition
  • Service history
  • Any damage or defects
  • Outstanding finance

4.2 Valuation Validity

Online valuations are valid for 7 days from the date of issue. We reserve the right to adjust the valuation based on market conditions or if the vehicle does not match the description provided.

4.3 No Obligation

Obtaining a valuation does not obligate you to sell your vehicle to us, nor does it obligate us to purchase your vehicle.

5. Vehicle Inspection

5.1 Physical Inspection

All vehicles are subject to a physical inspection before final purchase. This inspection will verify:

  • Identity and ownership
  • Mechanical condition
  • Cosmetic condition
  • Mileage accuracy
  • Service history
  • Outstanding finance or liens

5.2 Price Adjustments

If the vehicle differs from the description provided, we reserve the right to adjust the offer price or withdraw the offer entirely.

6. Sale of Vehicle

6.1 Ownership Requirements

You must be the registered keeper and legal owner of the vehicle or have proper authority to sell it.

6.2 Required Documentation

To complete the sale, you must provide:

  • V5C registration document (logbook)
  • Valid photo ID
  • Proof of address
  • All keys and security codes
  • Service history (if available)
  • MOT certificate (if applicable)

6.3 Outstanding Finance

If your vehicle has outstanding finance, you must:

  • Declare this during the valuation process
  • Provide settlement figure from the finance company
  • Authorize us to contact the finance company

We will settle outstanding finance directly with the lender from the sale proceeds.

7. Payment

7.1 Payment Method

Payment is made via bank transfer (BACS) to the account details you provide.

7.2 Payment Timing

Payment is typically made within 24 hours of collection, subject to:

  • Successful vehicle inspection
  • Receipt of all required documentation
  • Clearance of any outstanding finance
  • Verification of ownership

7.3 Payment Disputes

Any disputes regarding payment must be raised within 48 hours of the scheduled payment date.

8. Collection and Delivery

8.1 Collection Arrangements

We will arrange collection at a mutually convenient time. You must ensure:

  • The vehicle is accessible
  • You or an authorized representative is present
  • All required documents are available

8.2 Cancellation

Either party may cancel a collection appointment with at least 24 hours' notice.

9. Warranty and Liability

9.1 Seller's Warranty

By selling your vehicle to us, you warrant that:

  • You have the legal right to sell the vehicle
  • The vehicle is free from encumbrances (unless declared)
  • All information provided is accurate and complete
  • The vehicle has not been stolen or written off

9.2 Our Liability

We will exercise reasonable care when handling your vehicle and personal information. However, we are not liable for:

  • Pre-existing damage to the vehicle
  • Items left in the vehicle after collection
  • Delays beyond our reasonable control
