Getting Started

ReadySold makes selling your car simple and fast. Just enter your registration number and a few details about your car, get an instant valuation, and schedule a free collection. We handle all the paperwork and pay you on the same day.

You'll need your vehicle registration number, current mileage, and information about your car's condition. The whole process takes less than 2 minutes.

Yes! Our advanced valuation system provides an accurate quote in seconds, completely free with no obligation to sell.

Valuation & Pricing

We use real-time market data, industry pricing guides, and current demand to provide the most accurate valuation. Our system considers your car's make, model, age, mileage, and condition.

Your online quote is guaranteed as long as your car matches the description provided. If there are any discrepancies during inspection, we'll discuss adjustments with you before proceeding.

Payment & Collection

We pay on the same day we collect your car. Payment is made via bank transfer and typically arrives within a few hours of collection.

Yes, we offer completely free collection anywhere in the UK. We'll arrange a convenient time and come to your home or workplace.

We pay via instant bank transfer (BACS). This is the safest and most secure method for both parties.

Documents & Paperwork

You'll need your V5C logbook (registration document), valid photo ID, proof of address, and any service history or MOT certificates you have.

You can apply for a replacement V5C from the DVLA. This typically takes 5-7 working days. We can still provide a valuation in the meantime.

Yes! We handle all DVLA notifications and transfer of ownership. You'll receive confirmation once everything is complete.

Vehicle Condition

Yes! We buy cars in any condition, including those with cosmetic damage, mechanical issues, or even non-runners. Just be honest about the condition for an accurate quote.

While not required, a clean car helps with the inspection and may result in a better valuation. However, we buy cars in any condition.

We buy cars with or without a valid MOT. The condition is factored into your valuation, so just let us know during the quote process.

Finance & Outstanding Payments

Yes! We can help settle your outstanding finance as part of the sale. We'll contact your finance company directly and handle the settlement.

We pay the finance company directly from your sale proceeds, and you receive any remaining balance. If you're in negative equity, we'll discuss options with you.

A settlement figure is the total amount needed to pay off your finance agreement early. You can get this from your finance company, and we'll help you through the process.
