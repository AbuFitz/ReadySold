ReadySold is a managed private-sale service. We handle the preparation, presentation, enquiries, and viewings while you keep full control of pricing and the final decision to sell. All sales are private transactions between buyer and seller — we provide facilitation, preparation, and support.

Sell-Ready Service Fee: £495 (paid upfront, non-refundable) — Covers inspection, photography, listing, and buyer management whether or not the car sells.

Success Fee: £395 (only if sold) — Flat fee payable only if a sale completes through ReadySold.

Optional preparation, servicing, or repairs are quoted separately and only carried out with your approval.
